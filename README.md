# Accessibility in Human-Computer Interaction (Algeria Context)

## Overview
This project is a literature-based research study analyzing accessibility challenges in digital systems, with a focus on the Algerian context.

## Key Focus Areas
- Accessibility barriers (sensory, motor, cognitive)
- User-centered design (ISO 9241-210)
- Accessibility standards (WCAG)
- Inclusive design practices

## Key Insights
- Many digital platforms lack accessibility considerations
- Limited awareness of WCAG standards
- Opportunities for inclusive and localized product design

## Why This Matters
This study highlights the importance of accessibility in building inclusive digital products and provides insights relevant to product managers and designers.
